Welcome to the website of the Climate Dynamics and Data Science group at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, led by Cristi Proistosescu.

## What We Do

We study the dynamics of Earth's climate system and how it responds to natural and anthropogenic forcing. Our work spans coupled climate dynamics, paleoclimate, heat wave physics, climate forecasting, and climate economics. We combine physical theory, climate model simulations, observational data, and statistical methods to understand climate variability and change across timescales.

## Notes Section

This notes section serves as an informal space where we share updates on ongoing research, reflections on papers, technical tutorials, and conference reports.

To add a new post, create a Markdown file in the `posts/` folder and register it in the `POSTS` array inside `index.html`.
